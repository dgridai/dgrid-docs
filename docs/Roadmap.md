# Development Roadmap

## 2025 Q1-Q2: Infrastructure Construction Phase

- Development of DGrid core modules (DGridNode, Proof of Quality algorithm, GridRPC, etc.)
- Design and implementation of the PoS Quality Proof algorithm
- Design of network architecture and economic model
- Development of node presale module

## 2025 Q3-Q4: Testnet Phase

- Launch of official website and whitepaper
- Completion of seed round financing
- Release of DGridNode Validator Node V1
- Launch of node presale

## 2026 Q1-Q2: Mainnet Launch & Expansion Phase

- Release of DGridRPC and integration of enterprise-grade mainstream models
- Launch of node mining and incentive pool
- Integration of x402 Protocol, multi-chain payment integration, and DGridRPC access to multi-chain ecosystems
- Introduction of DGrid ecosystem projects to drive the network into a period of scalable growth

## 2026 Q3-Q4: Governance Improvement & Ecosystem Expansion Phase

- Launch of node staking
- Activation of on-chain governance and voting system, establishment of AI DAO 1.0
- Launch of Model & Agent Market and DGrid Browser
